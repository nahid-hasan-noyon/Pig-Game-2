GAME RULES:

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.

- In each turn, a player rolls two dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his current score.

- But, if the player rolls 1, all his current score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn

- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his current score gets added to his total score.

After that,it's the next player's turn.

- A player looses his ENTIRE score when he rolls two 6. After that, it's the next player's turn.

- You can chose the winnig score which is by default 100.
